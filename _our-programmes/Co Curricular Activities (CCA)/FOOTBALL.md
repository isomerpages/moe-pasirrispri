<p>Pasir Ris Primary Football focuses on the holistic development of our
pupils. Under the guidance of our experienced teachers and coaches, we
strive to enable our pupils to attain their maximal potential in the sport.
This is achieved through our comprehensive football development programme
that is built on sportsmanship, discipline, confidence and perseverance.
Our football programme is also differentiated to meet the different needs
and aspirations of our pupils.</p>
